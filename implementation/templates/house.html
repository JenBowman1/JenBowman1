    • Advanced House Pricing Predictor

    Finding an accurate estimate for the price of your home can be a challenge. Many different variables impact the final price of the home, and are often difficult to quantify. House Pricing Algorithims help people find a fitting price for their home and help those who plan to buy a home in the future. This house pricing application was built using a gradient boosting model, using a dataset with 79 explanatory variables, each describing a unique feature of residential homes in Ames, Iowa. It successfully predicts the price of a home in this location with an 87.5% R^2 Score (coefficient of determination) and an RMSE (root mean square error) of $14,576. This algorithim was built for a competition on kaggle.com, in which it placed within the top 15% of submissions.
